## Why a CLA Exists

The sethir-marketplace is licensed under the Elastic License 2.0 (ELv2) and may be
re-licensed commercially in the future. For the Licensor (Rahul Sethi) to have the
legal flexibility to offer commercial licenses, sublicense, or change the public
license, all contributors must grant Licensor sufficient rights over their contributions.

Without a CLA, a contributor's code is copyrighted by that contributor, and the
Licensor would be unable to re-license or sublicense it to commercial customers —
effectively freezing the project's commercial options.

**By submitting a Contribution, you confirm that you have read and agree to the terms
of whichever CLA form applies to you (Individual or Corporate).**

---

## How to Submit Your CLA

Before your first Pull Request is merged:

1. Copy the appropriate form (Individual or Corporate) from below.
2. Fill in all fields marked [FIELD].
3. Email the completed, signed form to: rahulsethi@github (subject: "CLA — [Your name]")
   OR open a Pull Request that adds a signed copy to `docs/cla-signatures/[your-github-handle].md`.
4. The Licensor will confirm receipt and note your CLA status in the repository.

Subsequent contributions from the same contributor under the same entity do not
require a new CLA unless you change employer (Corporate CLA) or your legal name changes.
